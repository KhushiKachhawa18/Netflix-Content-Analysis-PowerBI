# 📊 Netflix Movies & TV Shows Analysis (Power BI Dashboard)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Netflix’s content library using Power BI to uncover key insights into content distribution, genre trends, ratings, and global availability. The goal is to transform raw data into actionable insights that can support content strategy and business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

With thousands of movies and TV shows available, it becomes difficult to:

* Understand content trends across years
* Identify popular genres and ratings
* Analyze global content distribution
* Make data-driven decisions for content investment

This project solves these challenges by providing an interactive dashboard for better visibility and analysis.

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Source: Kaggle
* Dataset Link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/paramvir705/netflix-dataset

The dataset contains information about:

* Titles (Movies & TV Shows)
* Genres
* Release year
* Ratings
* Country
* Duration

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboarding)
* Excel / CSV (Data Source)
* Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Total Movies vs TV Shows distribution
* Content added over time (trend analysis)
* Top genres on Netflix
* Ratings distribution (TV-MA, PG, etc.)
* Country-wise content availability
* Duration analysis for movies and shows

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Movies dominate Netflix content compared to TV Shows
* Significant growth in content addition after 2015
* Drama and Comedy are the most popular genres
* Majority of content is rated TV-MA (mature audience)
* The United States contributes the highest number of titles

---

## 💼 Business Impact

This analysis can help:

* 📌 Content strategists identify trending genres
